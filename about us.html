About Us

Who We Are

Community Cleanup is a platform dedicated to organizing and promoting community cleanup events. Our mission is to foster a cleaner and greener environment by bringing together volunteers and community members to tackle local cleanup projects.

Our Mission

We believe that a clean community is a happy community. Our goal is to reduce litter, promote recycling, and enhance the natural beauty of our neighborhoods through collective action and environmental stewardship.
